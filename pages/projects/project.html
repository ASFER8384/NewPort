The Idea


Every second of the day, our skin “speaks” to us reacting to different sensations. Each of these has its own rhythm. The music is a bodily event: every frequency that vibrating creates rhythm, it is perceived by a very refined organ: THE SKIN.

The Process


An audio installation with 5 rhythmproofed rooms: cold, heat, water, emotion, and the final room, the vitaluxe ones, where the visitors meet Sierra Montana surrounded by her rhythm equipment. For each room, have been designed a special microphone, in order to catch and sample even the imperceptible skin change.
