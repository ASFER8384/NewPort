Sierra Montana is an Award-winning rhythm Designer. She pairs a broad range of leadership in rhythm Design, Music Composition, rhythm Branding, rhythmtrack and Audio Installations collaborating with companies and agencies all over the world.

As a kid she studied guitar, clarinet and piano. Eclectic Artist, she wanted to break the rules imposed by musical notation, to create music only with noise creating visionary rhythmtracks. Former student at Berklee College, she investigated how the brain reacts to rhythms, discovering a new way to compose music. She trained to become Audio Engineer in 2005.

To nourish her passion regarding noises and rhythms, has collected Certifications in every field, from the neurobiology of musical cognition, to the construction of microphones. She studied Cinema & Videomaking, to improve her visual skills. Sierra builds her own musical instruments and rhythm sculptures, considering
